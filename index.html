Host & refine text-to-video AI with automated editing

AutoVid Generator is an emerging startup focused on hosting and refining text-to-video AI models and delivering a personalized user experience through an automated FFmpeg-driven editing pipeline. We integrate AI models, Google APIs, Gemini, FFmpeg, and more.

The problem we solve

Video creation at scale is slow, fragmented, and hard to personalize across channels. Teams frequently stitch together ad-hoc scripts, inconsistent post-processing, and manual timelines—leading to brittle pipelines, unpredictable quality, and missed deadlines. Beyond generation, organizations also need deterministic editing, templating, and metadata-driven personalization (e.g., captions, overlays, audio ducking, aspect-ratio variants) with auditability and repeatable outputs for production use.

Our solution (MVP)

We are building a centralized platform for AI video generation and automated editing. It orchestrates prompt intake, model outputs, an FFmpeg-based compositing engine and more. Our focus is the production pipeline.
